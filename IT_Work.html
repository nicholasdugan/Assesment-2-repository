To reach where he is Johnathan studied a Bachelors degree in Network Communication and in order to progress further he completed a masters in IT Administration and Security.

Day in the Life of a Web Designer | Flux Ran Segall

What kind of work is performed by the IT professional?

Ryan Segall is a web designer, developer, business owner and youtuber. Predominately he works as a web designer where he works with a team of 2 other website developers to create high end websites for large businesses, normally he takes on 1-2 jobs every quarter which allows him enough time to properly devote to development. As well as this he also works as a youtuber where he teaches people how to improve their skills in web design and also shows people his life working in the field. As a result of being a popular youtuber he also does a lot of promotions such as speeches and events which are targeted towards a tech based market.


What kind of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they IT professionals, clients, investors or the general public?

As a business owner he has to engage with multiple different groups of people, the main one is clients where he is the first point of contact for all engagements between the clients and his team. He also engages with many different IT professionals as he focuses mainly on web design and in some cases requires help from other professionals with a specific set of skills who can help with a problem that he may be facing with the website. As a result of his youtube channel he has to also deal heavily with the general public in order to generate future sales for his business as well as also keep his channels following strong.


Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

Before the Covid 19 pandemic Ran was working in a shared office space in Tel Aviv this allowed his team to best access high quality facilities for a relatively cheap overall cost. Currently however he has been working from his home office as Israel is under stricter lockdown conditions when compared to Australia.


What aspect of their position is most challenging?

Ran says that the most difficult part of his job comes when dealing with his clients. As a result of being a small business he has to choose which clients he wants to work with this normally comes from a pool of at least 5 potential businesses. Therefore he has to spend lots of time trying to understand everything about the business and the project in order to make his choice which is fairly time intensive. Furthermore once he has chosen a business because of the cost of the project he has to ensure that the clients are being informed of all relevant details and have to consult them on every choice he makes which although it can be reasonably expected it does distract him from performing his work and this is why he believes it's his most challenging aspect of his business.



A Day in the Life of an IT Pro: IT Manager Johnathan Dalia

What kind of work is performed by the IT professional?

Johnathan works as an IT manager for a medium sized American trucking company therefore he is in charge of all IT related jobs that a present within the business. His primary role is to set up, configure and maintain all data links between the business this can be visual, audio or purely data connections mostly between warehouse locations, customers and with truck drivers out on the road.


What kind of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they IT professionals, clients, investors or the general public?

Johanthan mainly associates with other IT professionals as he is in charge of the IT department and therfore has to lead and direct the team under him to perform tasks correctly. He is also the first point of contact for all other members of the company if they have an issue that needs resolving so he interacts with clients when there has been a IT issue that needs resolving or explaining to the clients.


Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

He spends the majority of his time based out of the main office in New Jersey however as his role requires ensuring that all data links are setup and working efficently he will often travel to the businesses 4 other data centers based in Illinois, Texas, south Carolina and Louisiana however this is usually for a short amount of time and the majority of his work is at the main office. With Covid 19 he is able to perform much of his work remotely and only spends a portion of his time in the offices to solve any problems that require a physical presence.


What aspect of their position is most challenging?

Johnathan states that learning new skills is the most challenging part of his job. He is qualified in Network Communications as well as IT administration and has certificates in various different areas however when entering this role he was required to learn new processes and systems on the fly such as managing and implementing an enterprise resource planning system migrating from a DOS system without any previous experience. Therefore his biggest challenge is implementing new programs and learning the skills to operate them in a time sensitive industry without any outside guidance.

Due to the nature of work she has meetings between different groups of designers as well as sessions with the engineer and product manager partners.
